Privacy Policy for Happy Steps

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Welcome to Happy Steps. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information. By using Happy Steps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Happy Steps does not collect, store, or share any personal information on external servers. All data you enter into the app—such as your child’s name, activities, or milestones—is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Photos, media, or files

2. How We Use Your Data

Data stored in Happy Steps is used solely within the app to help parents manage and track their children’s daily routines and milestones. We have no access to your data.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device’s operating system. You may delete all stored data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option inside the app settings.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Happy Steps does not:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use advertising networks or tracking tools
  • Include third-party SDKs that monitor usage

Your data stays private and offline.

5. Children’s Privacy

Happy Steps is designed to help parents manage their children’s routines. The app does not collect personal data from children, and no online activity or accounts are required. As such, we comply with child privacy laws, including the COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
